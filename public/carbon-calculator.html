Carbon calculator

At Powershop, being carbon neutral is kind of a big deal. All the electricity we supply to our customers is 100% carbon neutral (at no extra cost). No need to opt in or pay more – it’s done for you, behind the scenes. We’re the only power company in Australia to be accredited 100% carbon neutral by the Australian Government.

Whether you’re a Powershop customer or not, we’ve put together this simple, handy calculator to help you see your household’s carbon emissions generated from electricity usage at your home or business (this is important because about one third of Australia’s greenhouse gas emissions are from electricity emissions).
